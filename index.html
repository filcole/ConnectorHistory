Connector Status

This site is a consolidated list of power platform connectors and actions in a computer-friendly format.

Please refer to the Microsoft's official connector reference documentation or Connector Review for full details.

  • All.json - a list of all connectors and actions
  • Deprecated.json - a list of all connectors and actions that have been deprecated
  • NonDeprecated.json - a list of all connectors and actions that have not been deprecated

All Brand Names are owned by the respective brand owners and are used here only in reference to their Power Automate connectors.
